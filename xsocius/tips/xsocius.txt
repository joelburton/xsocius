Beta testers are the most awesome people in the world.
You can use your Gmail account (or other Jabber account) to play puzzles online.
You can unlock encrypted puzzles using the "Unlock Puzzle" option in the Puzzle menu.
You can switch between filling out in pen and pencil using the "Use Pencil/Pen" option in the Puzzle menu.
Some puzzles require special characters or words in a square; if your puzzle does, you can enter these with "Enter Special Answer" in the Edit menu.
You can use the timer to test your speed skills.
If you want to prevent yoruself from using any help, you can turn on "Tournament Mode" options in the preferences.
